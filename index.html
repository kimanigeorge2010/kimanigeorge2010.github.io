About Kimani George

Great creative work is never an accident. It is the product of clear strategy, deep craft, and the courage to say something that matters.

I am a Creative Producer and Brand Strategist with over ten years of senior-level experience leading high-impact campaigns across East Africa — and, increasingly, for international brands.

My work sits at the intersection of strategic thinking and visual craft. I've led national television campaigns reaching millions of viewers, developed integrated brand strategies for some of the region's most recognised names, and competed on a global stage — winning creative briefs commissioned by Fortune 500 companies including Ferrero through the eYeka platform.

In recent years, I've expanded my practice into AI-driven visual development — combining emerging creative technologies with the rigorous strategic foundation I've built over a decade. The result is work that moves faster, reaches further, and retains the craft that separates memorable campaigns from forgettable ones.
